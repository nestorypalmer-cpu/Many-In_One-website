WWE SmackDown Results – December 13, 2025

United States Championship On The Line, Mixed Tag Main Event, Oba Femi Appears & More

The night opens backstage as SmackDown GM Nick Aldis meets Cody Rhodes and informs him that Drew McIntyre has sent a legal letter claiming an unsafe working environment.

Aldis says he'll visit Drew’s home later this week. Cody crumples the letter and heads to the ring.

Cody Rhodes is in the ring talking about Drew, saying he’s not afraid and will beat him down. Suddenly, his opponent for Saturday Main Event Oba Femi shows up.

Oba reminds Cody that he’s worried about the wrong guy and should fear the “greatest NXT Champion.”

The segment ends with a face-off as both men hold up their respective titles.

Ilja Dragunov (c) vs. Tommaso Ciampa – United States Championship

Midway, Candice LeRae interferes and cheap-shots Dragunov while Johnny Gargano distracts the ref.

Ciampa hits a running knee and nearly wins, but Dragunov kicks out and rolls him up for the win.

Winner (and still): Ilja Dragunov

DIY immediately starts beating down Ilja Dragunov after the match, but Carmelo Hayes runs out to make the save. He stands tall with Dragunov to end the segment.

The Wyatt Sicks appear as Uncle Howdy addresses Solo Sikoa and MFT.

Howdy mocks Sikoa’s claim that MFT runs SmackDown, reminding him who holds the Tag Team Titles. He dares them to come get the titles if they can.

The music hits but MFT only appears on the screen. Sikoa talks briefly and confirms they’re coming for the Tag Titles next Friday.

A tribute video plays showing celebrities like Snoop Dogg, Jelly Roll, and others wishing John Cena good luck in his final match tomorrow.

Lash Legend vs. Alexa Bliss

A solid back-and-forth match. Eventually, Lash Legend connects with the Lash Extension after a dominant run for the pin and win.

Winner: Lash Legend

Jade Cargill is shown backstage attacking Michin before making her way to the ring.

In the ring, Cargill begins trash talking, but Michin stumbles out holding a kendo stick and starts beating Cargill with it.

They brawl back and forth until Cargill gains control and finishes with a Chokeslam, standing tall to end the segment.

Rhea Ripley & Damian Priest vs. Zelina Vega & Aleister Black (Mixed Tag Team Match)

A competitive back-and-forth match. Eventually, Vega and Ripley tag in and battle it out. Vega counters a Rip Tide into a Code Red and goes for the pin, but Ripley kicks out.

The finish sees Ripley land a Razor’s Edge on Vega, followed by a Rip Tide for the win, while Priest takes out Black.

Winners: Rhea Ripley & Damian Priest

Ripley and Priest celebrate the victory to officially close out the night’s show.
