WWE Raw Results

Date: 08-12-2025

AJ Lee & AJ Styles Defends, Hooded Man Attacks, Knight Gets Destroyed, More...

GUNTHER opens the show with a promo. He talks about his upcoming match with John Cena, calling Cena a tough opponent. GUNTHER vows to make Cena do what he swore he never would — "give up".

#MatchResults

IYO SKY vs. Kairi Sane

Rey Mysterio heads out for his match with Finn Balor, but suddenly Logan Paul attacks him from behind. Rey fights back until the same masked man from Survivor Series shows up, delivering a Superkick and a Curb Stomp to Rey. He escapes through the crowd as security gives chase.

Back in the ring, Logan Paul hits Rey with brass knuckles to finish him off. LA Knight rushes out to make the save, and Paul retreats. Knight challenges him to a fight later tonight.

Dragon Lee & AJ Styles (c) vs. The War Raiders
World Tag Team Championship Match

After a back-and-forth battle, Dragon Lee gains momentum with a dive over the top rope to take out Erik. AJ Styles, the legal man, hits a Phenomenal Forearm on Ivar from the top rope for the win.

Winners (and still champions): Dragon Lee & AJ Styles

After the match, Kofi Kingston, Xavier Woods, and Grayson Waller surround Lee and Styles. Suddenly, the music hits — it's Jimmy and Jey Uso!

The Usos announce their return to the tag division, warning all teams. They take out Kofi and Woods, while Waller escapes. The Usos then stare down Styles and Lee as they raise their titles high to end the segment.

Stephanie Vaquer is being interviewed in the ring, bragging about destroying Nikki Bella at Survivor Series. She claims she's better than every woman in the locker room.

The music hits — Nikki Bella interrupts, shading both Vaquer and the fans, demanding a rematch. Suddenly, Raquel Rodriguez storms out, pulls Nikki from the ring, and destroys her on the outside.

Raquel grabs a mic and tells Vaquer to name the time and place — she’s coming for the Women’s World Championship.

Roxanne Perez vs. Lyra Valkyria

Midway through the match, Liv Morgan interferes with a cheap kick to Valkyria. Perez capitalizes and hits Pop Rox for the pin and victory.

Winner: Roxanne Perez

LA Knight vs. Logan Paul

Midway through the match, Knight gains control, beating Paul down near the announce table. Suddenly, the hooded figure appears and drops Knight with a brutal Curb Stomp on the announce table.

Paul capitalizes, hitting a Frog Splash from the top rope for the pin and win.

Winner: Logan Paul

Bron Breakker, Bronson Reed, and Logan Paul take turns attacking and destroying Knight after the match.

Paul Heyman speaks, promising Breakker will defeat CM Punk on January 5 to become the new World Heavyweight Champion. Breakker says he wants the 2011-2012 version of Punk, not the "nice guy" persona.

Backstage, the assault continues, ending with Reed delivering a Tsunami on Knight on top of a car. Adam Pearce and officials rush in as the show fades out.
