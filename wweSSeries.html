After much back and forth, Ripley‚Äôs team gained the upper hand. SKY hit a Swanton Bomb off the top rope, taking out Legend, Jax, Sane, and Asuka with a trash can. Winners: AJ Lee, Alexa Bliss, Charlotte Flair, IYO SKY, and Rhea Ripley

Intercontinental Championship: John Cena (c) vs. Dominik Mysterio

Midway through the match, Cena accidentally knocked out the ref. Cena locked in the STF on Dominik. Finn Balor and McDonagh attacked Cena but were fought off with a Double AA. Dominik tried to hit Cena with the title but Cena dodged and hit an AA. A new ref was called but instead Liv Morgan returned, slapped Dominik, hugged Cena, then shocked everyone by dropping Cena with a low blow. Dominik capitalized with a 619 and Liv hit Cena with the title. Dominik finished with a Frog Splash for the win.

Women's World Championship: Stephanie Vaquer (c) vs. Nikki Bella

Back and forth action that spilled to the floor. Vaquer took control with a clothesline and delivered the Devil's Kiss on the announce table. Back in the ring, she hit another Devil's Kiss followed by a moonsault off the ropes to pin Nikki and retain the title.

Winner (and still): Stephanie Vaquer

Men's WarGames Match: Drew McIntyre, Logan Paul, Bronson Reed, Bron Breakker, and Brock Lesnar vs. Cody Rhodes, Jimmy Uso, Jey Uso, CM Punk, and Roman Reigns

High intensity match with Superman Punches, Spears, Suplexes, F5s, GTS, Superkicks, and Brass Knuckle hits. Punk seemed set to win after putting out Logan with GTS, but a hooded figure climbed the cage and superkicked Punk, then hit a Curb Stomp before leaving. Breakker capitalized with a big spear and pinned Punk for the win.

Winners: Drew McIntyre, Logan Paul, Bronson Reed, Bron Breakker, and Brock Lesnar

Roman Reigns looked unhappy but showed sportsmanship by helping Punk up. Cody Rhodes stood on his own as the three men stared each other down to close the show.
